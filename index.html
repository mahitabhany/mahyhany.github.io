OUR STORY

Established in 1999, Delicious Inc. gave birth to the Café at the start of the millennium with the opening of its first café in Zamalek, Cairo, Egypt in the year 2000. Since its opening, it has set out to effect a dramatic change in the coffee sphop concept in Egypt and lead the Market to a new era. It came to the scene and helped develop the market of the western style café, thus paving the way for many international chains to join in. However, it continued to pioneer, educate & lead the market.

Coffee

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. The genus Coffea is native to tropical Africa, and Madagascar, the Comoros, Mauritius and Réunion in the Indian Ocean.The plant was exported from Africa to countries around the world and coffee plants are now cultivated in over 70 countries, primarily in the equatorial regions of the Americas, Southeast Asia, India, and Africa.
